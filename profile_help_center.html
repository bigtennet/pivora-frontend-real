Help Center

  • Forgot the PIN code processing method for platform transactions

    To reset your PIN code, please contact our customer support team through the in-app chat or email support@coinwybi.com with your account verification details.

  • What is the value of the stop loss and profit setting in opening a position? How should it be set?

    Stop loss and take profit settings help manage risk. Set stop loss at a price where you want to limit losses, and take profit at your target price. These can be adjusted in the order settings before confirmation.

  • How to reduce contract risk?

    Use proper position sizing, set stop-loss orders, diversify your trades, and avoid over-leveraging. Regularly monitor market conditions and your open positions.

  • What does the quantity in the contract opening mean?

    Quantity represents the number of contracts you're trading. Each contract corresponds to a specific amount of the underlying asset (e.g., 1 BTC contract = 1 BTC).

  • How is the handling fee in the contract calculated?

    Fees are typically calculated as a percentage of the order value. For example, if the fee is 0.05%, a $1,000 order would incur a $0.50 fee. Check our fee schedule for exact rates.

  • Notes on forced liquidation

    Liquidation occurs when your margin balance can't cover losses. Maintain adequate margin, monitor positions, and consider using stop-loss orders to prevent liquidation.

  • What are the contract trading rules?

    Our contracts follow standardized specifications including size, expiration (for futures), and settlement procedures. Refer to the contract details before trading.

  • What are limit order and market order?

    Limit orders execute at your specified price or better. Market orders execute immediately at current market prices. Limit orders provide price control while market orders guarantee execution.

  • What does the risk level of contract transactions represent

    Risk level indicates your account's liquidation risk based on current positions and market volatility. Higher levels mean greater risk of liquidation.

  • Why is currency exchange necessary?

    Exchange allows converting between cryptocurrencies and stablecoins like USDT for trading pairs not directly available or to manage volatility exposure.

  • How to buy USDT through the platform?

    Navigate to Buy Crypto, select USDT, choose your payment method, enter amount, and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the purchase.

  • Why does the converted amount of assets change?

    Converted values fluctuate with market prices. The displayed amount reflects real-time conversion rates between the assets.
